How We Started

Stop the Harassment was founded in 2023 after a series of tragic incidents involving fake loan app harassment led to multiple suicides across India. Our founding team, comprising cybersecurity experts, legal professionals, mental health counselors, and social activists, came together with a shared mission: to prevent such tragedies and create a safer digital environment.

The initiative began when Rajesh Kumar, a cybersecurity expert, lost a close friend to harassment from fake loan apps. This personal tragedy motivated him to research the problem deeply and develop solutions. He was soon joined by other like-minded professionals who had witnessed similar cases in their respective fields.

Since our inception, we have:

  • Helped over 5,000 harassment victims
  • Conducted awareness sessions for 2 lakh+ people
  • Collaborated with 15+ police departments
  • Assisted in 200+ legal cases
  • Prevented an estimated 50+ potential suicide cases

ğŸ”¬ Our Approach

1. Prevention Through Education

We conduct comprehensive awareness programs targeting students, working professionals, and vulnerable communities. Our sessions cover digital literacy, financial safety, and early warning signs of harassment.

2. Immediate Crisis Support

Our 24/7 helpline provides immediate assistance to harassment victims. We offer psychological support, legal guidance, and help with reporting procedures.

3. Technology Solutions

We develop AI-powered tools to detect fake apps, verify legitimate lenders, and predict harassment patterns. Our research contributes to building safer digital ecosystems.

4. Legal Advocacy

We work with law enforcement, provide legal aid to victims, and advocate for stronger regulations against digital harassment and predatory lending.

5. Policy & Research

We conduct research on digital harassment trends, work with policymakers to strengthen regulations, and collaborate with international organizations.
