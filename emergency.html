📋 What to Do RIGHT NOW

STEP 1: Ensure Your Safety

  • Don't Panic: Take deep breaths, you will get through this
  • Tell Someone: Inform a trusted friend or family member immediately
  • Stay Connected: Don't isolate yourself
  • If Suicidal Thoughts: Call 9152987821 (Vandrevala Foundation) immediately

STEP 2: Document Everything

  • Screenshots: Take screenshots of all threatening messages
  • Call Recordings: Record harassment calls (if legal in your state)
  • App Details: Note down the name and details of the harassment app
  • Timeline: Write down dates and times of each incident
  • Save Loan Documents: Keep all loan agreements and transaction records

STEP 3: Stop the Harassment

  • Block Numbers: Block all harassment numbers immediately
  • Report Spam: Report numbers as spam on Truecaller
  • Change Privacy Settings: Make social media profiles private
  • Don't Share OTP: Never share OTP or bank details
  • Uninstall Apps: Remove the harassment app from your phone

STEP 4: Report the Crime

  • Online Complaint: File complaint at cybercrime.gov.in
  • Local Police: Visit nearest police station with evidence
  • RBI Complaint: Report to RBI at cms.rbi.org.in
  • App Store: Report the app on Google Play Store/App Store
