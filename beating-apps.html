Dangerous Beating Apps

Learn about harmful apps used for harassment and intimidation

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Warning

The following apps have been identified as tools for harassment, intimidation, and in some cases, have led to tragic outcomes including suicide. If you or someone you know is being harassed through these apps, please seek help immediately.

1. Beat Master Pro

Type: Mock threatening app

Danger Level: Extremely High

How it's harmful: Creates fake police complaint certificates, threatening legal documents, and intimidating messages that appear official.

Reported Cases: Over 150 harassment cases reported, 12 suicide cases linked in 2023.

Real Case: A 28-year-old software engineer from Bangalore committed suicide after receiving continuous fake legal notices through this app.

2. Pressure Point

Type: Psychological harassment tool

Danger Level: Very High

How it's harmful: Sends automated threatening messages, creates fake court summons, and generates false arrest warrants.

Reported Cases: 89 documented harassment incidents, 8 mental health emergencies.

Real Case: A college student in Delhi attempted suicide after receiving fake arrest warrants through this app for 2 weeks.

3. Force Call Pro

Type: Call bombing and harassment

Danger Level: High

How it's harmful: Makes unlimited calls to target's number, spoofs caller ID to appear as police/court, plays threatening audio messages.

Reported Cases: 200+ call harassment complaints, 15 hospitalization cases due to stress.

Real Case: An elderly person in Mumbai had a heart attack after receiving 500+ threatening calls in one day.

4. Scare Tactics

Type: Intimidation and extortion tool

Danger Level: Extremely High

How it's harmful: Creates deepfake videos, generates fake evidence, sends morphed photos to family members.

Reported Cases: 67 blackmail cases, 22 family harassment incidents, 5 suicide attempts.

Real Case: A teacher in Chennai faced family ostracization after harassers sent morphed photos to her relatives using this app.

5. Collection King

Type: Debt harassment simulator

Danger Level: Very High

How it's harmful: Generates fake loan agreements, creates false debt records, sends threatening collection notices.

Reported Cases: 134 fake debt harassment cases, 18 wrongful collection incidents.

Real Case: A housewife in Hyderabad was harassed for a loan she never took, leading to her attempting suicide.

6. Terror Text

Type: Message harassment platform

Danger Level: High

How it's harmful: Sends anonymous threatening messages, creates fake group messages from family/friends, spoofs official communications.

Reported Cases: 178 message harassment complaints, 25 cases of family disputes caused.

Real Case: A business owner in Pune faced bankruptcy after fake messages about his company were sent to all his clients.
